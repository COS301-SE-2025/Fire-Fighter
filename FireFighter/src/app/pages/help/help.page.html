FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

How long does FireFighter access last?

Firefighter access is time-bound and typically expires after 4-8 hours, depending on the emergency type. Access is automatically revoked when the issue is resolved.

What happens if my request is denied?

Denied requests include detailed reasoning. You can appeal through the escalation process or contact the emergency support hotline for critical issues.

Can I extend my FireFighter session?

Extensions require additional approval and justification. Submit a new request with the original ticket reference and updated reasoning.

How are my actions audited?

All FireFighter activities are logged with timestamps, user attribution, and action details. Audit trails are immutable and reviewed regularly.
